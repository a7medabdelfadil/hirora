### Project Description

**Hirora** is an AI-powered recruitment platform designed to streamline the hiring process.  
The platform leverages artificial intelligence to match candidates with suitable job opportunities, analyze CVs, and assist employers in making data-driven hiring decisions.

The goal of Hirora is to improve recruitment efficiency, reduce bias, and connect the right talent with the right opportunities.
